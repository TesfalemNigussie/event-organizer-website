about us

we will give a very special celebration for you

VIP Events is a design, planing and events rental company specializing in clients with distinguished taste who want nothing less than the absolute finest in quality and service.

Our team of professionals who have 10+ years of experience can create events for an audience of any size. For any special moment. We want to bring your vision to life! Our team of creatives add imagination, while our experienced planners work on logistics to execute a flawless experience. From venue, décor, branding, transportation, 360 photo booths, marquee letters, red carpets and runner rugs or and any of our sought after event rentals; we are sure to leave you and your guests with nothing but good memories! Oh, and we will have the photos to prove it!

For rentals, we handle all the work, from start to finish, to ensure a smooth experience on your day. We will drop off the equipment, set it, operate it and pack it up at the end of your event. We do all the work, you just enjoy the good times! All machines and products are well-maintained, and guaranteed to satisfy your needs.

We hope to work with you soon to bring your dream celebration to life!
